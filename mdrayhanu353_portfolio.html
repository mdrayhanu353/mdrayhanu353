About

THE DESIGNER
BEHIND THE WORK

I'm Rayhan Uddin — a CGI & 3D Designer, Graphic Visual Storyteller, and AI-Workflow Automator based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

My work lives at the collision of typography, 3D design, photo compositing, and AI-powered visual generation. Every piece I create is built to tell a story — whether it's letters carved into mountain roads, names submerged in digital oceans, or 3D text that people can literally stand on.

Currently at BDCOM Online Ltd as an Executive Assistant while pursuing a Diploma in Computer Science, I bring both operational precision and a restless creative vision to every project.
